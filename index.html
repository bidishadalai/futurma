Our system uses an Arduino to detect the distance of nearby objects through ultrasonic signals. These real-time measurements are displayed on a custom-built website designed specifically for this project. We aimed to make the data clear and engaging, so when an object is detected, the numbers fluctuate quickly or stabilize based on its proximity. Lights respond to these changes, creating an interactive and futuristic security experience. Additionally, we included a buzzer that increases in pitch as an object gets closer, adding an extra layer of alertness.

To make the experience even more engaging, we experimented with playful soundtracks like the "Friends" theme and the "Minions Banana Song." This added a fun twist to our security system, merging technology with a touch of entertainment.
